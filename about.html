About Our School

A growing private educational institution nurturing discipline, creativity, and moral values in learners.

Our Foundation

Katale Progressive Nursery and Primary School is a growing private educational institution offering both nursery and primary education in Jomayi Estate, Nalumunye, Kampala.

We are committed to providing a strong academic foundation. With a highly supportive learning environment, the administration ensures that pupils receive personalized attention and guidance from our dedicated teachers.

Our focus is not solely on academics, but on cultivating moral discipline and igniting the creative spark within every child, preparing them for the future with confidence.

Inspiring Minds, Building Character

Why Choose Katale Progressive?

  • Strong Academic Performance

    We boast a track record of commendable performance in national examinations, driven by a rigid curriculum and test preparation.

  • Qualified and Caring Teachers

    Our staff provides personalized attention, ensuring no pupil is left behind in their academic journey.

  • Safe and Accessible Location

    Located securely in Jomayi Estate, serving the Nalumunye community with an easily accessible, secure campus.

  • Focus on Discipline & Values

    Emphasis is placed on shaping not just the intellect, but the moral compass of every child we educate.
